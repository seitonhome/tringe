What We Do

Comprehensive services, one trusted partner

From the first sketch to the final finish, we cover every trade your project needs.

Design, Engineering & Consulting

Architectural, electrical, hydro-sanitary, structural and geotechnical studies and design — including deep foundations and concrete quality control.

Civil Works Construction & Maintenance

Housing, buildings, roads and urban infrastructure — from new construction to remodels, repairs and facility maintenance.

Project Planning

PMI-standard project management: scope, cost, schedule, quality, risk and resources — fully under control.

Urban Planning

Urban planning and design focused on sustainability, mobility, and the quality of public and private space.

Geotechnical Studies

Slope stability analysis, soil improvement, and geotechnical monitoring — building on solid, well-understood ground.

Project Oversight

Technical, legal and financial oversight to ensure the project meets every contractual commitment.

Earthworks

Site grading, slope cutting, compacted fill and mechanical excavation with specialized heavy equipment.

Electrical Installations

Low and medium voltage networks, substations, panels and lighting — for residential, commercial and industrial projects.

Structural Steel & Welding

Design, fabrication and maintenance of steel structures, with MIG/MAG, TIG, plasma arc and other welding processes.
